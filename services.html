Orthopedics & Joint Replacement


Joint Replacement is a surgery performed to relieve pain from Arthritic and damaged joint. In this damaged articular surface is replaced by artificial prosthesis. Joint prosthesis can be made up of Metal, Ceramic or Polyethylene. Knee and Hip are common joints which require Joint Replacement.

Total Knee Replacement

Total Knee Replacement is operation performed to relieve pain in arthritic knee. With increase in life expectancy, more number of senior citizen demand a better quality of life. A painful knee can seriously hamper a person’s capacity to walk freely and make him dependent not only on drugs to relieve pain but also on others for mobility. Knee replacement relieves pain in severe arthritis. It is commonly done in patients suffering from osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis.

Total Hip Replacement

Total Hip Replacement is well established treatment method to relieve pain from affected hip. It also significantly improves quality of life and mobility of patient. In this procedure affected portions of joint i.e. Femoral head and acetabulum are replaced with artificial components to form new joint.

Breast Clinic


Breast pain (mastalgia) can be described as tenderness, throbbing, sharp, stabbing, burning pain or tightness in the breast tissue. The pain may be constant or it may occur only occasionally, and it can occur in men, women and transgender people.

Breast pain can range from mild to severe. It may occur:

  • Just a few days a month, in the two to three days leading up to a menstrual period. This normal, mild-to-moderate pain affects both breasts.

  • A week or longer each month, starting before a period and sometimes continuing through the menstrual cycle. The pain may be moderate or severe, and affects both breasts.

  • Throughout the month, not related to a menstrual cycle.
